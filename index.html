About Moi

Allow me to introduce myself, my name is Jasmin.


I have big plans for my future in coding. I plan on expanding my portfolio to include client side web applications tailored to fulfill my clients needs. I use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript among other tools to create or modify products that will satisfy the needs of clients. Using and creation code allows me to deploy applications in a cross-platform, cross-browser environment. I plan to eventually begin helping all sorts of businesses update their current or new websites to meet modern web standards.

I am a Junior Full-Stack Developer with a demonstrated history of working in the hospitality industry so catering to the needs of a client is right up my alley. I know how important the user/customer experience is to a business. I am skilled in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Microsoft Word, and Online Content Creation. I swear by problem solving, Team Motivation, and Analytical Skills, as they each play a role in making things run smoothly. I am a strong support professional, having earned my High School Diploma focused in Communications from Noblesville High School. Going forward, Eleven Fifty Academy has given me the tools and platform to thrive in my career as a Full-Stack Web Developer.

I come from a large family, my mom being the 14th of 16 children. So I take pride in having the ability to empathize with people from all walks of life. Having come straight out of the customer service industry before tech, I have a special understanding of the user/costomer experience before even typing my first code. I am very detail oriented but be that as it may, everything must have order and I feel the same way when it comes to delivering a product. Using the tools and foundation giving to me during my training at Eleven Fifty Academy, I now can develop for and supply the needs of clients with a new set of expertise.
